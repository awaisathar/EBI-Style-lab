{{#markdown}} ### What is this, exactly? The EBI Style Lab has guidance on how and what should be used to build visually and textually compliant EMBL-EBI websites, documents, and texts. It's partly about reinforcing our shared identity, but it's mostly about leveraging the best tools to build things faster and with fewer bugs, errors, and user experience issues. ## Contents of the EBI Style Lab {{/markdown}}
